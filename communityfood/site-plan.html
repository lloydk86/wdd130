Kapande Foundation – Community Food Preservation

Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

The Kapande Foundation’s Community Food Preservation initiative empowers communities through sustainable food preservation practices. Visitors learn techniques to reduce food waste, improve nutrition, and strengthen local resilience. The website serves as both an educational resource and a community engagement platform.

Audience

Target audience includes community members, volunteers, students, educators, and organizations interested in sustainable food practices. The site is user-friendly and responsive, ensuring accessibility across desktop and mobile devices.

Content

Home Page

Title: Community Food Preservation Initiative
Tagline: “Preserve food. Empower communities.”

Content:
The home page introduces the Kapande Foundation’s mission. It explains why food preservation matters, highlighting the reduction of waste, improvement of nutrition, and community resilience. It also emphasizes the social and environmental benefits of shared preservation knowledge.

Sections:
- Why preservation matters (nutrition, waste reduction)
- Community benefits (resilience, skill-sharing)
- Call-to-Action: “Explore Methods” button linking to Methods page

Images:

Methods Page

Title: Preservation Methods

Content:
This page teaches three main preservation techniques: fermentation, drying, and canning. Each method is explained with step-by-step instructions, benefits, and safety tips. An interactive "Top 5 Safety Guidelines" section ensures users practice safe preservation at home.

Sections:
- Fermentation: Benefits, examples, images
- Drying: Sun drying, dehydrators, storage tips
- Canning: Equipment, safety, shelf life
- Safety Guidelines Button

Images:

Get Involved Page

Title: Get Involved

Content:
This page encourages users to volunteer, attend workshops, and participate in community events. It provides a sign-up form and contact details for engagement. Visitors can learn how to contribute to local food preservation efforts while building community connections.

Sections:
- Volunteer Opportunities
- Event Calendar
- Sign-Up Form
- Contact Information and Social Media Links
