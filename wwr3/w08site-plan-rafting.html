Overview

Purpose

This website promotes our whitewater rafting business by showcasing the thrill, safety, and natural beauty of our guided river adventures. It serves as a central hub for trip details, booking options, and educational content about the rivers we explore.

Audience

Our audience includes adventure-seeking individuals and families aged 18–55. They value nature, travel, and physical activity, and need clear trip info, safety assurances, and mobile-friendly booking. The site must be responsive, fast, and visually immersive.
